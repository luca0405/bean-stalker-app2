Bean Stalker Coffee - Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 26, 2025

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information:

  • Account information (username, email, phone number for SMS notifications)
  • Order history and preferences
  • Payment information (processed securely through Square and RevenueCat)
  • Device information for push notifications

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Process and fulfill your coffee orders
  • Send order status notifications (SMS via Twilio, push notifications)
  • Manage your account and credit balance
  • Improve our app and services
  • Process in-app purchases and credits

3. Information Sharing

We share your information only with trusted service providers:

  • Square: Payment processing and order management
  • RevenueCat: In-app purchase processing
  • Twilio: SMS notifications for order status
  • Omnisend: Contact management for SMS campaigns

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Secure HTTPS connections
  • Encrypted data storage
  • Password hashing and secure authentication
  • PCI-compliant payment processing through Square

5. Notifications

We use notifications to enhance your experience:

  • Push Notifications: Order status updates, promotional offers (can be disabled)
  • SMS Notifications: Critical order updates via Twilio (optional)
  • Local Notifications: In-app status updates and reminders

6. In-App Purchases

Our app offers in-app purchases for coffee credits through RevenueCat, which handles:

  • Purchase processing and receipts
  • Subscription management (if applicable)
  • Refund processing through Apple App Store/Google Play policies

7. Location Data

We may request location access to:

  • Show nearby coffee shop locations
  • Optimize pickup and delivery options
  • This feature is optional and can be disabled in your device settings

8. Data Retention

We retain your information as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You may request account deletion at any time.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, update, or delete your personal information
  • Opt out of notifications and SMS communications
  • Request a copy of your data
  • Delete your account and associated data

10. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes through the app or email.

12. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at:

Email: privacy@beanstalker.com
Support: support@beanstalker.com

This privacy policy is designed to comply with App Store guidelines and privacy regulations including CCPA and GDPR where applicable.
